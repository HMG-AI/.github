# HMG-AI code of conduct

HMG-AI is committed to a professional, inclusive, and harassment-free collaboration environment in repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, events, and other project spaces.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, direct, and constructive.
- Critique ideas, code, and outcomes rather than people.
- Welcome questions and differing experience levels.
- Protect private, security-sensitive, and customer information.
- Accept responsibility, correct mistakes, and help repair harm.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate disruption, repeated bad-faith demands, retaliation, and publication of private information are not accepted.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `monkseekee@gmail.com`. Include relevant links and context, but do not amplify harmful material. Maintainers will limit disclosure to the people needed to investigate and will address conflicts of interest.

Responses may include a private warning, content removal, temporary participation restrictions, repository or organization access removal, and referral to the relevant platform or authorities when necessary. Decisions consider impact, intent, repetition, remediation, and community safety. Retaliation against a reporter or participant in an investigation is prohibited.
